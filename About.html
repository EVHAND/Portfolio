Evhan DIDIER in 2025

I'm a Narrative Designer and Game Designer with a passion for creating engaging stories and fun gameplay. My focus is on designing game worlds that feel alive and immersive, with narratives that pull players in and keep them invested.
I work on developing characters, dialogue, and branching storylines that complement gameplay mechanics, making sure everything works together to create a smooth and enjoyable experience. Whether it's coming up with interesting quests, building lore, or designing core gameplay systems, my goal is to make every part of the game feel connected and meaningful.
I enjoy collaborating with teams to bring ideas to life and am always looking for new ways to blend storytelling with interactive design to create something unique.
